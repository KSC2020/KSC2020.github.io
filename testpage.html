About Us

We are a group of Kashmiri Pandit scientists and technologists sharing a common dream - to nurture, motivate and sustain the passion for science & research among the young & budding scientists within our community.

We want to encourage students and researchers to pursue excellence with global standards, through guidance on best practices for independent and collaborative research.

Over time, we hope to establish a free, equitable platform for interested people within the community to network, engage, encourage, mentor & collaborate with each other in a life-long learning model.

In the process, we would also like to connect our young scientists to our traditional knowledge systems and the values of enquiry - sustained through the vicissitudes of time.

Modern scientific and technological pursuits are increasingly complex and interdisciplinary in their texture. Cross-disciplinary collaborations will be of primal importance for making progress on the big problems of future societies. We hope that our endeavour will reach out to the creative potential in our community at large, and result in first rate contributions at a global scale.

We have planned following activities as a start

  • Curate a Database of Kashmiri Pandit Researchers
  • Distinguished Lecture Series
    • Navreh Series
    • Diwali Series
  • Popular Lecture Series
    • Guru Purnima Series
    • Makara/Shishir Sankranti Series
  • Annual Prizes For Outstanding Research
